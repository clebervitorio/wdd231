Kombine with your party is a company focused on festive celebrations (birthdays, weddings, corporate parties, etc.). Our mission is to provide a pleasant environment through the rental of Kombis, transporting the celebrant(s) and providing your guests with the experience of having participated in the event through the services we offer. Our headquarters are in Lisbon, Portugal, and our vision is to celebrate, remember and party.
